About EnoGen AI

EnoGenAI is the first biotech/AI platform that uses yeast genomics, AI, and reverse engineering to design precision fermentation for wine. We enable producers to replicate and personalize sensory profiles through custom yeast mixtures derived from AI-based predictive models and genomics. We’ve developed a proprietary dataset and AI algorithms that power our platform, creating a defensible advantage at the intersection of science, data, and taste.

Technology

Using genomic data, reverse engineering and AI models, we map and predict wine fermentation outcomes to achieve precision fermentation.
